About Guru Ponmalar Karthick

Guru Ponmalar Karthick is an accomplished Bharatanatyam artist trained in the Pandanallur style. With over 15 years of dedicated training in classical dance, she has successfully completed all grade examinations, showcasing her unwavering passion and mastery in the art form. For the past 3+ years, she has been mentoring aspiring dancers with utmost dedication.

Full Biography

About: Mrs. Ponmalar Karthick has been immersed in Bharatanatyam for over 15 years, mastering the Pandanallur style with deep discipline and devotion. Her passion for the art reflects in her teaching and stage performances alike.

Achievements: Performed at national dance festivals, Guinness World Record holder (Pondicherry).

Programs: Natyanjali, Nrithya Kalotsav, Pongal Mahotsav, Temple Utsavs.

Awards: Kalaimamani Junior, Youth Icon Award, Natyakala Ratna.
